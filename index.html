How does Learn Link work?

Our priority is to make our platform incredibly easy to use, so that our users can focus on what matters to them the most.

01/ Import

Upload your pdf file onto our platform. Learn Link is designed to handle multiple documents at once, so dont hold back!

02/ Analysis

As soon as you upload your document, Learn Link's AI will begin working on analysing the file, to learn content and prepare for your questions

03/ Chat

When analysing is finished, Learn Link's chat bot will begin talking to you. Ask it any question, and the bot will provide accurate responses using the data you provided
